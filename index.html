I'm a Computer Science student at Georgia Tech who's passionate about colors, comics, programming, beautiful interfaces, and doing good work.

Things I've made:

  • Tiff's Random Color Box: An interactive random color generator.
  • Cookie Clicker Essay: An essay for English class on the game Cookie Clicker, presented self-referentially.
  • Jacobi's Method Calculator: Javascript calculator for diagonalizing symmetric matrices using Jacobi's Method.
  • PokeDS: A comprehensive Pokemon site aiming to host a multitude of unique and high quality features.
  • Nameless Comic: A collection of various short, whimsical one page comics hand drawn in marker and ink.
  • The Aywas and Wajas Wikis: Wikis for the virtual pet sim games, Aywas and Wajas.

Projects I've been a part of:

  • Aywas: A virtual petsite focusing on the ability to highly customize and breed your pets.
  • Invisible Ink: A simple online steganography application made during HackIllinois 2015.
  • Explorer: An experimental universe-exploring game using procedurally generated content.
